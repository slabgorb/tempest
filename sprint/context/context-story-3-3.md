## Problem
Spikes (laid down by Spikers, persisting per-lane) are harmless during normal
play, but the design requires them to be lethal during the end-of-level **warp**.
Story 3-2 landed the warp geometry switch; this story makes spikes crash the Claw
when the camera flies down the tube during that warp. Without it, the warp is a
free ride and the spike mechanic has no consequence.

**Spec references** (`docs/superpowers/specs/2026-06-24-tempest-clone-design.md`):
- L94: `spikes: number[]` — per-lane spike height (`0` = none).
- L143–145: "Spikes persist in lanes between/within levels. Harmless during
  normal play, but during the end-of-level warp (camera zooms down the tube)
  hitting a spike on your lane kills you. Bullets shorten/clear spikes."
- L159–160: "the warp: the camera flies down the tube (player still steers to
  dodge spikes), then the next geometry/color appears."
- L182: "Collision … spike↔player-on-warp."

## Technical Approach
_High-level only — TEA/Dev own implementation detail._
- Spike↔Claw collision is checked **only while in warp mode** (`mode === 'warp'`),
  inside the pure deterministic core (`src/core/sim.ts` warp step). It must obey
  the hard boundary: no DOM/canvas, no `Date.now`/`Math.random`; time enters via
  `dt`, randomness via the seeded RNG in `GameState`.
- Collision is tube-space lane+depth overlap: as warp progress advances the camera
  down the tube, the Claw crashes when it reaches a spike on **its current lane**.
- On crash, reuse existing death/lives handling (enter `dying`, decrement a life,
  respawn after the pause, or `gameover` when lives are exhausted).

## Scope
- **In scope:** detecting a spike on the player's lane during the warp and
  crashing the Claw (death + life loss) via the existing death path; the player
  being able to steer to a spike-free lane to survive.
- **Out of scope:** Spiker enemy behavior / spike-laying (Wave 2), bullets
  shortening spikes (separate concern), the warp camera/render animation itself
  (3-2), and difficulty-ramp parameter tuning.

## Acceptance Criteria
_Starting set — TEA refines during RED._
1. During `warp` mode, when warp progress advances the camera past a spike on the
   player's **current lane** (height > 0), the Claw crashes: player enters the
   `dying` state and loses a life.
2. A lane with no spike (height `0`) never causes a crash during the warp.
3. Only a spike on the player's **current** lane is lethal — steering off a spiked
   lane before the camera reaches it lets the player survive the warp.
4. Crash outcome is **deterministic**: identical `GameState` (spikes[], player
   lane, warp timer) + identical inputs ⇒ identical crash result.
5. After the crash, lives/respawn follow existing rules — respawn on lives
   remaining, `gameover` when the last life is lost.
6. Collision logic lives in the pure core (`src/core/`) and adds no DOM/canvas or
   non-deterministic calls.
7. Surviving the warp (no spike on the player's lane, or dodged) completes the
   transition into the next geometry exactly as it does today.
